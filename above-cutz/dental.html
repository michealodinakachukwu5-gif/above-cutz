Treatments Offered

Our Dental Services

01

Dental Consultation & Check-Up

A thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and oral cavity. Includes a personalised assessment and treatment plan tailored to your dental health needs. The essential first step for any new patient.

02

Scale & Polish

Professional removal of plaque, tartar, and surface stains using ultrasonic and hand-scaling instruments. Finished with a fine polish that leaves your teeth smooth, clean, and noticeably brighter.

03

Teeth Whitening

A safe, professional-grade bleaching treatment that lifts deep staining and restores your smile's natural brilliance. Results are immediate and can brighten teeth by several shades in a single session.

04

Tooth-Coloured Fillings

Discreet composite resin fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural tooth colour. Effective for treating cavities while preserving a clean, natural appearance — no silver or metal in sight.

05

Tooth Extraction

Safe, careful removal of damaged, decayed, or problematic teeth under local anaesthesia. Performed with precision to minimise discomfort and support healthy healing of the surrounding gum tissue.

06

Root Canal Treatment

Expert removal of infected or inflamed pulp from within the tooth, relieving pain and preventing further infection. The treated tooth is cleaned, sealed, and restored — preserving it for years to come.

07

Dental Crown

A precision-fitted cap placed over a damaged or weakened tooth to restore its full shape, strength, and appearance. Crowns are matched to your natural tooth colour for a seamless, long-lasting result.

08

Dentures

Custom-fitted partial or full dentures crafted to restore a natural-looking, comfortable smile after tooth loss. Each set is designed for your unique bite and facial structure, ensuring a confident, secure fit.
